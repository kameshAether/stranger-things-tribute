The World of Stranger Things

Hawkins, Indiana hides dark secrets

In the sleepy town of Hawkins, Indiana, nothing is as it seems. Beneath the surface of this idyllic 1980s American town lies a dark conspiracy: a secret government lab conducting dangerous experiments, a rift to an alternate dimension, and supernatural entities that threaten to consume everything in their path. The future of the world is at stake.

🌌

The Upside Down

An alternate dimension mirroring our world, but twisted, dark, and filled with dangerous creatures. It's a place where time moves differently and the very air is toxic.

Dimension Paranormal Dark
🧠

Mind Flayer

A terrifying interdimensional entity that can possess and control living beings across dimensions. It feeds on negative emotions and grows stronger with each victim.

Entity Possession Evil
🔢

Vecna

A powerful psychic entity from the Upside Down. With a deadly ability to manipulate victims through their worst fears, Vecna is the show's most terrifying villain.

Psychic Villain Powerful
⚡

Hawkins Lab

Hawkins National Laboratory, where MKUltra experiments accidentally created the first gateway to the Upside Down. The source of all the town's troubles.

Government Secret Experiments
👁️

The Demogorgon

A terrifying monster from the Upside Down introduced in Season 1. It hunts through the rift using smell and telepathy, leaving destruction in its wake.

Monster Season 1 Deadly
🕷️

The Demobats

Flying creatures from the Upside Down that serve the Mind Flayer. They hunt in packs and are used to track down targets across dimensions.
